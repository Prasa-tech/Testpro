Academic Programs
Our flagship degree.

Built on evidence-based practice and rooted in compassionate care — our BPT program is designed to produce job-ready physiotherapists from day one.

Flagship Program
Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)
A comprehensive 4.5-year undergraduate program (4 years academic + 6 months compulsory internship) affiliated with Sambalpur University. Covers musculoskeletal, neurological, cardiopulmonary, and pediatric physiotherapy with extensive clinical exposure.

Our Mission

Serving Odisha through accessible, quality physiotherapy education.

Founded in 2004 in Sambalpur, SLB College of Physiotherapy was established with a single purpose — to make professional physiotherapy education accessible to students across Odisha, particularly those from rural and underserved backgrounds.

As a non-profit college, our surplus is reinvested into scholarships, infrastructure, and free community physiotherapy camps.
